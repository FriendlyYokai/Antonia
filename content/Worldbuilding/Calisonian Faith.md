The Calisonian Faith is a religion founded around the worship of the Architect, a creator deity who, manifesting himself using the First Martyr as a vessel, laid down a list of religious edicts and commandments in [[The Calisonian Codex]], colloquially known as "the Codex". Though all sects of the religion follow this book, being over a thousand years old means there's always varied interpretations of the text, with much room for debate.

## Religious Differences

The [[Orthodoxy of Calis]] is a very traditionalist branch of the Faith, and for over a thousand years it was more or less unopposed thanks to the political and military backing of the [[Empire of Derelia]]. With the collapse of the Empire, many different groups now call themselves followers of the Faith, some with radically different rules set out for their congregations. The main contender to the [[Orthodoxy of Calis|Orthodoxy]] is the [[New Universal Church of Calis]].
