The biggest ethnic groups in the continent of Beras are the Guernadine, Derelian, Arani, and Shegh ethnic groups. Together they make up roughly 70% of the population in the continent.

Outsiders to the continent usually have trouble telling the groups apart, as majority of members of all these groups have a fair to medium complexion with hair ranging from black, to blonde, to an occasional red. While most Berasians can tell each other apart based on a variety of facial features, outsiders tend to rely on eye color.
