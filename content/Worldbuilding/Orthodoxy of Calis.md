The Orthodoxy of Calis is a religious organization that leads the traditionalist believers of the [[Calisonian Faith]]. For most of recorded history, having enjoyed the backing of the [[Empire of Derelia]], it experienced little theological opposition. However, with the arrival of foreign cultures and religions into Beras (thanks to the recent advancements in Nyxium-powered airship technology which has drastically shrunk the world) as well as the collapse of the old Empires, several rivals have sprung up, namely the Berasian Republic's [[New Universal Church]], the foreign [[Heathen Faiths]], and newfound atheist tendencies with the population.
