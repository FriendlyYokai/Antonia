Volanea is a city in central [[Beras]], housing three million souls and acting as the capital of the [[Berasian Republic]]. It was the capital of the [[Empire of Derelia]] prior to the Empiire's collapse following the [[Fall of the Derelian Empire|Liberation War]] [[Fall of the Derelian Empire|]] in [[Calisonian Calendar|502 Twilight]].
