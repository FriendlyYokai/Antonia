The Berasian Republic, sometimes referred to as simple "Berasiy", formally known as The Most Serene Berasian People's Republic, is a union of 14 semi-autonomous provinces, created as a result of the Eternal Liberation Front's triumph over the [[Empire of Derelia]]'s forces in the [[Empire of Derelia#Fall of the Derelian Empire\|Midnighters' Revolution]].
