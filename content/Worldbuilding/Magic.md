Magic, is the usage of and control over [[Magic#Potential|Potential]] in order to achieve one's desired results. It differs from [[Miracles]] in that it is a deterministic system where Potential channeled in a specific way will always achieve the same results, barring outside interference. In more traditionalist areas such as the [[Kingdom of Derelia]] and parts of the [[Republic of Guernad]] it is referred to as "Magecraft" in order to differentiate it from the mythological [[True Magic]] which does not require Potential in order to affect the world.

## Potential

Potential is the name given by the Derelians to the unseen primordial force which fuels Magic. Its true origin is unknown to scholars, though the [[Orthodoxy of Calis]] claims that Potential is a form of divine investiture, power left over form the creation of the material world as we understand it.
